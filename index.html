About Me

My name is Derek Edgar G. Dacocos. I am 21 years old and was born on November 24, 2002. I live in Alaminos City, Pangasinan, a beautiful place known for its Hundred Islands National Park. I am passionate about anime and often spend my free time watching my favorite series. I also enjoy playing video games, which allows me to relax and immerse myself in different worlds. Sleeping is another favorite pastime of mine; it helps me recharge and stay refreshed. Besides these hobbies, I am also interested in exploring new technologies and staying updated with the latest trends in gaming and anime culture. I value my family and friends and love spending quality time with them.
